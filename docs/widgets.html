ISCP Widget Areas

ISCP provides professional widget areas for classic WordPress widgets and block-based widgets.

  • Sidebar
  • Blog Sidebar
  • Header Top Bar
  • CTA Before Footer
  • Footer Column 1 to Footer Column 4
  • Offcanvas Panel
  • WooCommerce Sidebar, available when WooCommerce is active

Recommended Uses

Use sidebars for search, categories, recent posts and lead CTAs. Use footer columns for navigation, contact details, service links and trust notes. Use CTA Before Footer for short conversion messages.

Widgets inherit the ISCP design system, colors, radii and spacing.
