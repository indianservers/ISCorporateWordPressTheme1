ISCP Conversion Tools

ISCP includes lightweight conversion helpers that can be enabled from Appearance > Customize > ISCP Theme Options > Conversion Tools.

Included Tools

  • Sticky CTA bar with mobile, desktop or all-device visibility.
  • Exit intent modal placeholder that appears once per session on desktop.
  • Scroll progress bar.
  • Reading time for blog posts.
  • Native share buttons for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Facebook and WhatsApp.

Accessibility Notes

The modal uses dialog semantics, a close button, Escape support and focus restoration. The tools do not use third-party tracking scripts.

Lead Forms

ISCP intentionally does not process contact form submissions inside the theme. Use your preferred form plugin such as WPForms, Contact Form 7 or Fluent Forms for storage, email delivery, spam protection and compliance features.

Performance

These tools use small native JavaScript behavior and CSS. They do not require Elementor, Divi, WPBakery or a heavy page builder.
