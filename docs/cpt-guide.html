CPT Guide

ISCP includes native post types for Solutions, Services, Industries, Case Studies, Testimonials, Team Members, FAQs and Pricing Plans.

Solutions

Use for products, software modules, SaaS offerings and business solutions.

Services

Use for professional services and deliverables.

Industries

Use for niche landing content.

Case Studies

Use client name, industry, result summary and project URL meta fields.

Testimonials

Use role, company and rating meta fields.

FAQs

Use the title as the question and content or excerpt as the answer.

Pricing

Use price, period, featured plan, CTA text and CTA URL meta fields.

Homepage Integration

The homepage sections query the latest CPT content first. If no relevant CPT posts exist, generic fallback cards are shown so the theme remains complete on a fresh install.

Permalinks

After activating the theme or adding CPTs, go to Settings > Permalinks and click Save Changes.
